Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Learn Ultimate is a simple learning tool for ultimate players and teams. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We may collect basic information you provide, such as your name, email address, team name, or feedback if you choose to contact us, sign up, or use a feature that asks for it.

We may also collect basic usage information, such as pages viewed, lesson progress, device type, browser type, and general performance data so we can improve the product.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve Learn Ultimate
  • Save progress or preferences if those features are available
  • Respond to messages or support requests
  • Understand which lessons and tools are most useful
  • Keep the site secure and working properly

3. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand usage, and improve the experience. If analytics tools are added, they should be used to understand product performance, not to sell personal information.

4. Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the site, such as hosting, analytics, email, or database providers.

5. Children’s privacy

Learn Ultimate is intended as a general sports education tool. If future features are designed for youth teams or players under the age required by applicable law, we will add appropriate consent and privacy protections before launch.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the product, improve the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve issues.

7. Your choices

You can contact us to ask about your information, request updates, or request deletion where applicable.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Learn Ultimate changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

9. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: hello@learnultimate.com
